Part 1 - Who is Kyle?

I am 37 years old and have been in the United States Air Force for 17 years, working in aircraft maintenance. I currently live in Hawaii and plan to stay there until I retire and further into the future. I have two kids who live in Georgia with their mother, Konnor (8) and Lillian (6); they, along with my fiancé and her son, make up my entire world and universe. The first picture is a collage of photos of my kids and myself; the next is me and my wonderful fiancé and love of my life, April. The last photo is all of us together as a family. We don't often get together with all 5 of us, but when we do, it's always some of the best days of my life.

My two favorite hobbies are golf and video games. Although I am not great at either, I enjoy both thoroughly. While home in Hawaii, I try to go out to golf at least every other weekend. Because Hawaii is a paradise, I get to do it year-round! There are rarely weekends when the weather is so terrible that I don't get to golf. One of my favorite things to do is golf with April; every time we are together, we try to get at least a round (or two) in! When the weather does not permit, or I need a slow and low-key weekend, you can find me on my PC, playing video games with my best friend, Devin.

Some of my favorite websites to visit are:

  • Reddit I spend plenty of spare time scrolling through reddit and exploring subreddits that interest me.
  • Steam Where I find great video games and deals for PC!
  • Pueakea Golf course This is one of me and my fiancé's favorite courses to play on Kauai.

I think the next step for my website is to start working with CSS and create something more appealing to the eye. I want to work with a sleek and modernized look, not like an old Facebook page. I have not decided on a color palette yet, but it will probably be something rather simple, maybe some pastel-type colors.
